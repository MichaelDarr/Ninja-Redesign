Pricing

Per Hour

Per hour billing is best for those who want to pay for exactly what they recieve. I will charge $20 per hour I work on the site, and sites can range anywhere from 5 to over 100 hours. Per hour lets you get what you pay for but does not offer a concrete budget, as I can never estimate perfectly.

Per Project

Billing per project is for those who would rather stick to their concrete budget. In this case, you would send me your project and I would send you a definite cost. This could range anywhere from $100 to over $1,000 depending on the scope of the work. Your budget, your project.
